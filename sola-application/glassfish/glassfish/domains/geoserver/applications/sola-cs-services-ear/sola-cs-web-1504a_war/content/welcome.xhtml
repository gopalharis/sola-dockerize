Welcome to SOLA Community Server

Using SOLA Community server you can register as a Community Member and view claims made by community members. User registration can be done online and once you complete registration Community Technologist will review it and approve. Upon approval a confirmation code will be sent to your e-mail address. You will then need to activate your registration request, by following the received link. Another way is to contact your Community Technologist and they will enroll you as a registered user.

All Community Member registrations are subject to review and approval by the Community Server Technologist and the Community Moderation Committee.

If you are already registered, use Login page to login into the system.
